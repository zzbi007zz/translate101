Exploring Vietnamese Culture and Language

Published on March 15, 2026 • 5 min read

Vietnamese is a fascinating language spoken by over 95 million people worldwide. The language has a rich history and unique characteristics that make it both challenging and rewarding to learn.

In this article, we'll explore some common Vietnamese phrases and their English translations: "Xin chào! Tôi rất vui được gặp bạn. Hôm nay thời tiết đẹp quá!" which showcases the melodic nature of the language.

Vietnamese uses six different tones, each changing the meaning of a word entirely. This tonal system is one of the first challenges learners face when beginning their journey with the language.
